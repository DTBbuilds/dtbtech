Common Questions

How long does cloud migration take?

Simple migrations take a few days. Larger business systems typically take 2 to 6 weeks. We plan everything upfront so there are no surprises.

Which cloud platform should I use?

It depends on your needs. AWS has the most services, Azure works best with Microsoft tools, Google Cloud excels at data and AI. We recommend the right fit after understanding your setup.

Will my data be secure in the cloud?

Yes. We configure encryption, access controls, firewalls and backup systems. Your data is often safer in the cloud than on local servers.

What does cloud setup and migration cost?

Pricing varies based on your current setup, data volume and complexity. We provide a fixed quote after an initial assessment. No hidden fees.
