Common Questions

Do I need separate apps for Android and iOS?

No. We use cross-platform tools that let us build one app that runs natively on both Android and iOS. Same look, same features, half the time and cost.

Do you publish to the Play Store and App Store for me?

Yes. We handle store account setup (under your business), submission, listing copy, screenshots and approvals. You don't need to learn the process, we've done it many times.

Can the app talk to my existing systems?

Yes. We connect your app to existing websites, POS, inventory, CRM, payment processors and more. If it has an API or a database, we can plug into it.

What about updates after launch?

Phones and operating systems change constantly. We offer ongoing support plans that cover bug fixes, OS updates and new features. You won't get stuck with a broken app a year from now.
