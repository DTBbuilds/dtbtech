Yes. Always. Your domain is registered in your business name, with your contact details on the WHOIS record. You can move it at any time. No hostage situations.

Yes. We do migrations regularly with zero downtime. We copy the site to our servers, test everything works, then switch the DNS. Visitors don't notice anything except that the site loads faster.

Our monitoring tells us within minutes and we get it back up. If something's broken, we restore from our daily backup. You almost never need to call us, but when you do, we answer.
