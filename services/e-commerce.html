Common Questions

Is this Shopify, WooCommerce, or something else?

We build custom stores using modern frameworks, or work with Shopify and WooCommerce if that's what suits you. We pick the platform based on your business, not the other way round. No platform lock-in we don't recommend.

Can you migrate my existing store?

Yes. We migrate products, customers, orders, URLs (with redirects so your SEO doesn't tank) and reviews from your old store to the new one.

How do payments work for Kenyan customers?

M-Pesa STK Push, Pesapal, Flutterwave, card payments — we integrate whatever works for your customers. Same goes for Australian shoppers: Stripe, Afterpay, PayPal, bank transfer.

Can my store connect to my POS or accounting software?

Yes. We sync inventory between your online store and physical POS, and push orders into Xero, QuickBooks or similar so your books stay accurate without manual data entry.
