Our Story

Founded in Nairobi in 2020, DTB Technologies emerged from a vision to bridge the digital divide in East Africa. What started as a small IT consultancy has grown into a comprehensive technology solutions provider, serving businesses across Kenya and the wider East African region.

Transforming businesses through technology, one solution at a time.

Our Mission

To provide cutting-edge technology solutions that empower African businesses to compete globally while contributing to the digital transformation of our continent.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's leading technology solutions provider, known for innovation, reliability, and excellence in service delivery.
