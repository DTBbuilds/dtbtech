Our Story

DTB Technologies started in 2020 as a small IT consultancy in Nairobi, helping local businesses get online and stay running. Today we operate from Sydney and Nairobi, building custom websites, mobile and desktop apps, and the hosting that keeps it all online. Different scale, same principles: solid software, honest work, clear pricing.

Solid software, built properly, delivered on time.
